## Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on myclawgo.com. We primarily use essential cookies necessary for the proper functioning of the website, and may use performance or analytics cookies to improve the service.

## What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owners.

## How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to ensure the website functions properly and to improve your experience. Essential cookies are always active and cannot be disabled, as they are required for core functionality.

**We do NOT use:**
- Targeting or advertising cookies
- Marketing cookies
- Social media cookies

## Types of Cookies Used

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled:

- **Session Cookies**: To maintain your login session and ensure functionality during your visit.
- **Security Cookies**: To protect against fraud and abuse.
- **Language Cookies**: To save your preferred language setting (e.g., NEXT_LOCALE).
- **Theme Cookies**: To save your theme preference (Light/Dark mode).

## Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

- Delete cookies from your device
- Block cookies by activating the setting in your browser that refuses the setting of all or some cookies
- Set your browser to alert you when you receive a cookie

Please note that blocking or deleting essential cookies may result in you being unable to use certain areas or features of our website.

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page.
