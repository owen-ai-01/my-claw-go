## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we process your personal data when you use myclawgo.com and the My Claw Go service — a platform that provides private, hosted OpenClaw AI workspaces. We adhere to applicable data protection laws including GDPR.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at **support@myclawgo.com**.

## 1. Controller

Responsible for the processing of your personal data:

- **Service**: https://myclawgo.com/
- **Email**: support@myclawgo.com

## 2. What Data We Process

### 2.1 Account and Communication Data

- **Account Data**: Email address, name (if provided), registration date, account status
- **Access Data**: Login times, session information
- **Communication**: Content of your support inquiries and our responses

### 2.2 Workspace Data

- **AI Conversations**: Messages sent to and received from your OpenClaw workspace agent, stored within your private workspace container
- **Workspace Files**: Files, memory, and configurations you create or modify in your private workspace
- **Usage Metrics**: Credits consumed, model usage, token counts for billing purposes

Your workspace data is isolated in your own private container and is not accessible to other users or shared with third parties except as described below.

### 2.3 Payment and Billing Data

Payment data (e.g., card details) is processed exclusively by our payment service provider **Stripe**. We only receive information necessary for billing: name, email, subscription plan, transaction ID. We do not store raw card data.

### 2.4 Technical and Log Data

- **Log Data**: IP address, date/time of access, requested URLs, HTTP status codes, referrer, user agent
- **Session Data**: Device type, language settings, approximate location (from IP), session duration

## 3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use **essential cookies** required for the basic functionality of our website and service, and may use **performance or analytics cookies** to improve the service.

We do not use marketing cookies.

**Essential Cookies:**

- **Session Cookies**: To maintain your login session
- **Security Cookies**: To protect against fraud and abuse
- **Language Cookies**: To save your language setting (e.g., NEXT_LOCALE)
- **Theme Cookies**: To save your theme preference (light/dark)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

## 4. How We Use Your Data

We process your data to:

- Provide and operate your private OpenClaw workspace
- Manage your account and subscription
- Process payments and apply credits
- Respond to support requests
- Ensure security and prevent abuse
- Improve the service

**Legal bases (GDPR):**
- **Art. 6(1)(b)**: Contract performance — to provide the services you requested
- **Art. 6(1)(f)**: Legitimate interest — service improvement, security, abuse prevention
- **Art. 6(1)(a)**: Consent — for optional communications

## 5. Sharing Data with Third Parties

We share your data only as necessary to operate the service:

- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **Cloud/Hosting Providers**: Server infrastructure
- **AI Model Providers (via OpenRouter)**: Your workspace agent's messages are routed through OpenRouter to AI model providers to generate responses. Messages are processed transiently and not used for training.

We do **not sell** your personal data.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained as long as your account is active, plus any legally required period
- **Workspace Data**: Retained as long as your account exists; deleted upon account closure
- **Log Data**: Up to 12 months
- **Billing Data**: According to applicable legal retention periods (typically 6–10 years)

## 7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

- **Access** (Art. 15): Request information about what data we process
- **Rectification** (Art. 16): Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Erasure** (Art. 17): Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
- **Restriction** (Art. 18): Request limited processing
- **Data Portability** (Art. 20): Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Objection** (Art. 21): Object to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Withdraw Consent** (Art. 7(3)): Withdraw consent at any time

Contact us at **support@myclawgo.com** to exercise your rights. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required by applicable law). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced on the website. The current version is always available on this page.
